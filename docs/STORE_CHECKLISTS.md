# Store submission checklists

## Apple App Store

- Bundle ID, Sign in with Apple capability, push notification entitlement, camera/location usage descriptions, associated domains, and privacy manifest confirmed.
- Account deletion is available in-app.
- Restore Purchases and Manage Subscription are visible.
- Annual-trial disclosure clearly states duration, price after trial, and auto-renewal.
- App Privacy answers disclose user content, coarse location verification, identifiers, diagnostics, and purchases without claiming collection of raw health data when it is not stored.
- Reviewer account and a private test circle are supplied in App Review notes.
- Age rating and community-guideline moderation answers are reviewed; default recommendation is 17+ pending counsel/store questionnaire.

## Google Play

- Package name, Data Safety form, Health Connect declarations, notification permission flow, account deletion URL, content rating, and user-generated-content policy complete.
- Billing products and base plans active in the testing track.
- Closed-test purchase, restore, expiry, grace period, and resubscribe scenarios pass.
- Background task and exact-alarm use are avoided unless demonstrably required.
